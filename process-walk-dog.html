The steps of walking dog

Steps to Follow

Follow these steps to ensure a safe and enjoyable walk for your dog:if you can't understand you can vast step four

  1. Put the leash on the dog.
  2. Open the door and step outside.
  3. Allow the dog to sniff around and explore.
  4. Start walking at a comfortable pace.
  5. Be aware of your surroundings and other animals.
  6. Use positive reinforcement to encourage good behavior.
  7. After the walk, remove the leash and give the dog water.

Walking your dog is not just a routine; it's a bonding experience that promotes health and happiness for both you and your pet. Enjoy the fresh air and the time spent together!
